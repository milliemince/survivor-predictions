It premiered on February 25, 2026, on <a href=\"/wiki/CBS\" title=\"CBS\">CBS</a> in the <a href=\"/wiki/United_States\" title=\"United States\">United States</a>, and it is the eighteenth consecutive season to be filmed in the <a href=\"/wiki/Mamanuca_Islands\" title=\"Mamanuca Islands\">Mamanuca Islands</a> in <a href=\"/wiki/Fiji\" title=\"Fiji\">Fiji</a>.<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-2\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-2\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>2<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup> Celebrating the show\u2019s twenty-fifth anniversary, and commemorating the show\u2019s fans, this season features game mechanics and production designs chosen by an online public vote which occurred during <i><a href=\"/wiki/Survivor_48\" title=\"Survivor 48\">Survivor 48</a></i>.\n</p><p>The cast was officially revealed on May 28, 2025, one week after the finale of <i>Survivor 48</i>. During the cast reveal, 22 out of the 24 players were announced, with two other players, both from the preceding <i><a href=\"/wiki/Survivor_49\" title=\"Survivor 49\">Survivor 49</a></i>, remaining a mystery until December 17, 2025, following the <i>Survivor 49</i> finale.<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-3\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-3\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>3<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-4\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-4\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>4<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup> With 24 contestants, this is also the largest cast the show has had to date. In addition, musicians <a href=\"/wiki/Zac_Brown\" title=\"Zac Brown\">Zac Brown</a> and <a href=\"/wiki/Billie_Eilish\" title=\"Billie Eilish\">Billie Eilish</a>, <i><a href=\"/wiki/Tonight_Show\" class=\"mw-redirect\" title=\"Tonight Show\">Tonight Show</a></i> host <a href=\"/wiki/Jimmy_Fallon\" title=\"Jimmy Fallon\">Jimmy Fallon</a>, and social media personality <a href=\"/wiki/MrBeast\" title=\"MrBeast\">MrBeast</a> are expected to make appearances during the season as celebrity fans who will impact the game.<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-5\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-5\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>5<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-50completecast&#95;6-0\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-50completecast-6\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>6<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>It is the fifteenth season to feature returning players, and the sixth to feature a cast consisting entirely of returning players, following <i><a href=\"/wiki/Survivor:_All-Stars\" title=\"Survivor: All-Stars\">Survivor: All-Stars</a>,</i> <i><a href=\"/wiki/Survivor:_Heroes_vs._Villains\" title=\"Survivor: Heroes vs. Villains\">Survivor: Heroes vs. Villains</a></i>, <i><a href=\"/wiki/Survivor:_Cambodia\" title=\"Survivor: Cambodia\">Survivor: Cambodia</a></i>, <i><a href=\"/wiki/Survivor:_Game_Changers\" title=\"Survivor: Game Changers\">Survivor: Game Changers</a></i>, and <i><a href=\"/wiki/Survivor:_Winners_at_War\" title=\"Survivor: Winners at War\">Survivor: Winners at War</a></i>.\n</p>\n<meta property=\"mw:PageProp/toc\" />\n<div class=\"mw-heading mw-heading2\"><h2 id=\"Production\">Production</h2><span Production\"><span>edit</span></a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span></div>\n<p>On December 15, 2023, <a href=\"/wiki/Manoa_Kamikamica\" title=\"Manoa Kamikamica\">Manoa Kamikamica</a>, the Fijian <a href=\"/wiki/Ministry_of_Commerce,_Trade,_Tourism_and_Transport_(Fiji)\" title=\"Ministry of Commerce, Trade, Tourism and Transport (Fiji)\">Minister of Commerce, Trade, Probst</a> announced that the 50th season would feature returning players.<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-8\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-8\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>8<span production team hosted a series of challenges for <a href=\"/wiki/Beast_Games_season_2\" title=\"Beast Games season 2\">season 2</a> of the <a href=\"/wiki/Amazon_Prime_Video\" title=\"Amazon Prime The series also featured a number of former <i>Survivor</i> contestants. As part of the crossover <i>Beast Games</i> presenter <a href=\"/wiki/Jimmy_%22MrBeast%22_Donaldson\" class=\"mw-redirect\" title=\"Jimmy &quot;MrBeast&quot; Donaldson\">Jimmy \"MrBeast\" Donaldson</a> is set to appear in an episode of <i>Survivor 50</i> as a celebrity fan, introducing a twist.<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-11\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-11\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>11<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup>\n</p><p>Beginning on February 9, 2026, <a href=\"/wiki/CBS\" title=\"CBS\">CBS</a> began airing 10 reruns of classic <i>Survivor</i> episodes programmed as \"The Road to 50.\" These episodes were key points in the <i>Survivor 50</i> cast's journey to the season. An array of people who have slept bled this ever-changing game design for the last 25 years. Because, really, <i>Survivor 50</i> is a representation of everyone who has ever played and the fans who've been with us for the entire time.\"<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-24\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-24\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>24<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup> In August 2024, Probst stated that the casting team started with a list of 200 previous players and had narrowed it down to 100 by that point in time.<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-25\" class=\"reference\"><a href=\"#cite_note-25\"><span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#91;</span>25<span class=\"cite-bracket\">&#93;</span></a></sup> When asked by Dalton Ross in October 2024 about players potentially in the casting pool taking offers for shows on other networks including <i><a href=\"/wiki/Australian_Survivor\" title=\"Australian Survivor\">Australian Survivor</a></i> and <i><a href=\"/wiki/The_Traitors_(American_TV_series)\" title=\"The Traitors (American TV series)\">The Traitors</a></i>, Probst said \"We've had many players call us and say, 'I only want to do <i>Survivor</i> again.'
